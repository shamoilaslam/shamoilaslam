## 🚀 What I'm Building

<div align="center">

### ⚖️ [Specter](https://github.com/shamoilaslam/specter_parsing_engine) — AI × Pakistani Law

</div>

> **The Problem:** Pakistan's legal system is bilingual (Urdu + English), structurally complex, and paper-heavy. Existing AI tools fail completely.
>
> **The Solution:** I'm building Specter — an AI platform that handles legal research, document drafting, and case review with deep understanding of Pakistan's specific legal structure and language.

**What makes this hard (and interesting):**
- 🌐 Bilingual legal corpus — Urdu & English mixed documents
- 🏛️ Unique Pakistani legal structure — different from Western legal AI training data
- 📄 Document parsing engine built from scratch for local court formats
- 🤖 Custom RAG pipelines for high-accuracy legal retrieval
